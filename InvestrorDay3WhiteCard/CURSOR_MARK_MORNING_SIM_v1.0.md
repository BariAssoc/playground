## SECTION 1 — NEW CANONICAL TERMS LOCKED IN THIS SIMULATION

### SIGNAL BOARD
The bar display showing Mark's overnight biometric stats. Temperature. Heart rate. HRV. Sleep score. Each metric shown as a colored bar (green = good). The Signal Board announces the Beacon. The Signal Board always comes first.

### BEACON BOARD
The 7-band corridor display. Appears on top of — or immediately after — the Signal Board. You cannot have a Beacon Board without a Signal Board first. The Signal Board announces the coming of the Beacon Board.

### COMMUNICATION COLOR — BLUE
All exchanges between Ollie and Mark (and between any AI and the client) are displayed in BLUE. Blue = universal communication color. Applies to both AI speech and client response bubbles.

### OLLIE LISTENING EXPRESSION
When Mark speaks — Ollie's face becomes Pearl White opaque. She is listening. The chat bubble covers the Ollie Space during this moment. No conflict. Pearl White = ears open, eyes soft.

### JOURNAL ENTRY BEHAVIOR — LOCKED
When a journal entry is confirmed:
✅ Checked → then DISAPPEARS
It does not stay on the board. It sweeps off — like a PowerPoint slide leaving the screen. Flying off the board. Gone. No need to stay once it is done.

## SECTION 2 — TWO OLLIE TONES

Ollie has two tones. Both used in this simulation. Zakiy must render both.

**TONE 1 — WARM**
Friendly. Encouraging. Personal. Uses Mark's name. Celebrates small wins.
Example: *"You did good, Mark."*

**TONE 2 — CLINICAL SOFT**
Still warm — but more precise. Used when a health signal needs attention. Not alarming. Just clear.
Example: *"Your HRV dropped 8% after the meeting. That is expected. Let's bring it back."*

**EXPRESSION RULE:**
- Tone 1 (Warm) → Blue bubble, Ollie in Pearl White or Green expression
- Tone 2 (Clinical soft) → Blue bubble, Ollie in Orange expression

## SECTION 4 — RENDERING RULES FOR ZAKIY

**JOURNAL CARDS:**
- Card slides in from right side
- Entries listed with ○ dot (pending)
- When confirmed → ✅ then sweeps off like a PowerPoint slide leaving screen
- Left to right. Fast. Clean.
- Card disappears when all entries done
- Nothing remains on the board

**SIGNAL BOARD:**
- Bars appear left to right
- Each bar colored: Green / Orange / Red
- Label on left. Value on right.
- Appears before Beacon Board always
- Beacon Board slides up beneath it

**BEACON BOARD:**
- 7 bands. Same as existing playground.
- Score dot in correct band.
- Appears after Signal Board.
- Cannot appear without Signal Board first.

**COMMUNICATION BUBBLES:**
- All bubbles: BLUE
- Ollie bubble: left side, Ollie icon
- Mark bubble: right side, no icon
- Both BLUE — universal color

**OLLIE EXPRESSIONS:**
- Pearl White breathing: calm/waiting
- Pearl White opaque: LISTENING to Mark
- Green: positive / momentum
- Orange: alert / clinical soft
- Transitions: smooth, 0.5 seconds

**MICROPHONE BUTTON:**
- Appears when Ollie finishes speaking
- Mark taps → Pearl White opaque
- Mark speaks → bubble appears
- Ollie expression returns when done
- Privacy rule: mic NEVER auto-opens

**TIME CARDS:**
- Black screen. White text only.
- "30 minutes later" / "15 minutes later" / "4 hours later" / "1 hour later"
- 2 seconds on screen. Then next scene.
