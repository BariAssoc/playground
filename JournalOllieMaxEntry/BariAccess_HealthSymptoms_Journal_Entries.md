## Ollie Tone Canon (locked April 24, 2026)

- **Never use reassurance language** ("no judgment", "no pressure", "no worries") — introduces the concept of judgment.
- **Acknowledge life context** ("lots going on today") — sees the patient's life, not just the checkbox.
- **Use passive voice** ("did X happen" not "did you do X") — removes the accusation.
- **Frame as anchor/support**, not surveillance.
- **Add "I'll log it either way" / "both are real answers"** — removes performance pressure.
- **Trust is shown, not declared.**
